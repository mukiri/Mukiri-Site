Why I Do This Work

Financial inclusion requires more than strategy — it requires systems that work.

I work at the intersection of technology, finance, and real-world implementation, helping financial institutions, fintechs, and development partners deploy digital financial systems that reach people who need them most.

Over the past decade, I have worked across Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Tanzania, Ethiopia, Zimbabwe, and Botswana, supporting banks, microfinance institutions, SACCOs, and agricultural value chains, delivering tangible impact for thousands of smallholder farmers and entrepreneurs.

Rather than staying in strategy rooms, I ensure fintech solutions move from boardroom vision to full operational deployment — from credit scoring platforms and digital lending infrastructure to mobile banking and agent networks.
